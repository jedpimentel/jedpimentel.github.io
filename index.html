I started programming while studying Industrial Engineering in the Dominican Republic. I learned enough C to do HackerRank challenges, then moved towards Web Development, teaching myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript using FreeCodeCamp. I later moved to Boston, and went through the Resilient Coders bootcamp, leading to a position as a full-time software developer.

After almost two years working at the Boston Illiquid Securities Offering Network (BISON), I took some time to work on personal code, spanning from Arduino stuff (telerobotics), to WebXR projects such as hand-tracking-based interfaces, and an assortment of UI component for interacting/navigating through VR websites.

I genuinly enjoy on more conventional sites/interfaces, and I'm happy working wherever I might be of use.
